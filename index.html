About Us

Sharma Enterprises, established in 1985, is one of India's leading companies involved in the design, development, and manufacturing of FRP components for Automotive, Industrial, Railway, and other applications.

Founded under the leadership of Mr. Ramnath Sharma, who has been involved in FRP manufacturing since 1982, we have built a strong reputation for quality and innovation.

In 2022, we expanded with our sister concern, Fibrelastic Composites, under the leadership of Mr. Ramnath Sharma and Mr. Nitesh Sharma.

With over 37 years of experience, our expertise lies in developing workable FRP solutions that offer enhanced life and minimum maintenance, earning the loyalty of thousands of users nationwide.
